Byrne Electric Motors successfully supplied new Grundfos NB pumps for Ireland’s largest aquatic centre — The National Aquatic Centre, right here beside us in Blanchardstown.

Home to Irish athletes and some of the most state-of-the-art facilities, these upgrades help support Ireland’s journey toward LA 2028.

Each pump was:

  • Designed to handle the harsh swimming pool environment
  • Built to drop seamlessly into existing pipework
  • Supplied with IE4 high-efficiency motors and insulated bearings for improved reliability and energy savings

All units were supplied and carefully stored on-site, ready for installation by the customer.
