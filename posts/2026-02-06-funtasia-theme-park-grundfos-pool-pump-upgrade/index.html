Each month, we shine a light on one of the projects we’re proud to be involved in—along with some of the brilliant Irish and international companies we work with. (Some projects are site-sensitive, so we can’t always share details.)

We’re kicking things off with one of Co. Louth’s finest waterparks: Funtasia Theme Park, Drogheda—entertaining families since 2007.

In January, Byrne Electric Motors supplied and supported the installation of brand-new GRUNDFOS NB150 (45 kW) and NB125 (18.5 kW) pumps—each designed and spec’d to handle harsh chlorinated swimming pool water, featuring IE4 motors, insulated bearings, and bronze impellers.

This full upgrade of the swimming pool plant will help ensure families and kids can keep enjoying Funtasia and its Pirates Cove attraction.

We currently have 3 major aquatic projects ongoing—if you have an upcoming project, why not get in touch? And if you’re looking for a great family day out, bring the kids to Funtasia.
