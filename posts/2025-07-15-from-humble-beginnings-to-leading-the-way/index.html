From Humble Beginnings to Leading the Way!

When we started Byrne Electric Motors in 2010, I bought a VW Transporter and €3000 worth of electric motors. We quickly sold the first batch of stock and bought another €3000 worth.

Fast forward 15 years, and as part of our expansion, Byrne Electric Motors has become one of the largest stockists in Ireland of Grundfos 2 and 3 pump booster sets. Coupled with our large stock of Grundfos Magna pumps, we have you covered for your heating, pressure boosting, and cooling needs.

In the first 6 months of this year, we’ve invested over half a million euros with plans to invest a similar amount by year-end. It’s a long way from where we started as we continue to grow and strive to become the largest supplier of electric motors in Ireland.

Yes, we have a long way to go in our pursuit to achieve this, but growth is growth, and investment has always been a mantra from day one.
