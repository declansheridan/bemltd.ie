Discover the Unusual Amtecs Electric Motor at Byrne Electric Motors

Ever wondered about the unique design of some electric motors? At Byrne Electric Motors, we introduce you to a distinctive Amtecs motor that’s not your usual setup. Notice the absence of a cowl and fan? That’s because this motor is an AOM (Air Over Motor) or what some might call a TENV (Totally Enclosed Non Ventilated).

This specialized smoke extract Amtecs motor is equipped to handle the heat. With a substantial 1.4m fan blade attached directly to its shaft, it keeps itself cool during operation. This particular model boasts a 30 kW 6 Pole Class H insulation and can withstand temperatures of up to 300 Degrees Celsius for 2 hours.
